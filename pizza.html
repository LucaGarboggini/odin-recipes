Pizza recipe

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 packet (2 ¼ teaspoons) active dry yeast
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm water
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

For the sauce:

  • ½ cup pizza sauce or tomato sauce
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • ½ teaspoon basil

For the toppings:

  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup black olives
  • Fresh basil leaves

Steps to make the pizza

Follow this steps to make a delicious pizza:

Step 1: Prepare the dough

  1. In a small bowl, mix the warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for 5-10 minutes until it becomes foamy.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the flour and salt.
  3. Add the yeast mixture and olive oil to the flour. Mix until a dough forms.

Step 2: Knead the dough

  1. Place the dough on a lightly floured surface.
  2. Knead for 8-10 minutes until smooth and elastic.

Step 3: Let the dough rise

  1. Place the dough in a lightly oiled bowl and cover with a clean towel.
  2. Let it rise for 1-2 hours until it doubles in size.

Step 4: Preheat the oven

  1. Preheat the oven to 475°F (245°C).

Note: If you have a pizza stone, place it in the oven while preheating.

Step 5: Shape the pizza

  1. Punch down the risen dough and transfer it to a floured surface.
  2. Roll it out into a 12-inch (30 cm) circle and place it on a pizza pan or baking tray.

Step 6: Add toppings

  1. Spread the tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving about a ½-inch border.
  2. Sprinkle the shredded mozzarella cheese over the sauce.
  3. Add your desired toppings.

Step 7: Bake the pizza

  1. Bake for 12-15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly.

Congrats! your pizza is ready!

Enjoy your delicious homemade pizza!
